### RWA Launchpad - Go-To-Market Strategy


## Executive Summary
- Product: Platform for tokenizing Real World Assets (real estate, commodities, equity, art, etc.)
- Target Markets: Asset owners seeking liquidity, investors seeking diversification, emerging markets with illiquid assets
- Primary Value Proposition: "Unlock liquidity from your real-world assets through compliant, secure tokenization"

Primary Segments

Have existing investor networks
Pain Points:

Limited to local investors

Value Proposition: "Raise capital faster with lower minimums and global investor access"

Segment B: SME Owners (High Volume)
Profile:

Companies valued $1M-$20M
Seeking growth capital
Open to alternative financing
Pain Points:

Traditional equity raises are expensive
Dilution concerns
Limited exit options for early investors
Value Proposition: "Tokenize equity to raise capital while maintaining control"

Segment C: Commodity Producers (Emerging Markets)
Profile:

Agricultural cooperatives

Commodity traders
Pain Points:

Working capital constraints

Limited access to international markets
